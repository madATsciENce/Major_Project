Top Sites to See in Kodaikanal

Kodai Lake

A stunning lake known for boating and scenic views.

Coakers Walk

A beautiful walkway offering panoramic views of the valley.

Berijam Lake

A serene lake surrounded by dense forests.

Silver Cascade Falls

A stunning waterfall that is a popular picnic spot.

Chettiar Park

A lush park ideal for relaxation and nature walks.

Guna Caves

A mysterious set of caves with stunning views.

Green Valley View

An observation point with breathtaking views of the valley.

Kodaikanal Solar Observatory

A scientific observatory with interesting exhibitions.

Astrophysics Observatory

A place to explore the wonders of the universe.

Bear Shola Falls

A beautiful waterfall perfect for nature lovers.
