Top Sites to See in Coorg

Abbey Falls

Popular waterfall surrounded by lush greenery.

Dubare Elephant Camp

Get up close with elephants in this interactive camp.

Raja's Seat

A scenic viewpoint with stunning views of the valley.

Iruppu Falls

A mesmerizing waterfall located in the Brahmagiri Range.

Nisargadhama

An island surrounded by the Kaveri River, perfect for picnics.

Talakaveri

The origin of the River Kaveri, with a temple and scenic views.

Omkareshwara Temple

An ancient temple known for its unique architecture.

Madikeri Fort

A historic fort offering insights into Coorg's heritage.

Chiklihole Reservoir

A peaceful spot with a reservoir surrounded by nature.

Pushpagiri Wildlife Sanctuary

A wildlife sanctuary home to various flora and fauna.

Transport Options

Coorg is well-connected to major nearby cities like Mysore and Bangalore. Here are the primary options for reaching Coorg:

Private Taxi

Enjoy a comfortable journey from Mysore or Bangalore with private taxis available at reasonable rates.

Bus Services

Frequent bus services operate from major cities to Coorg, offering a budget-friendly travel option.

Self-Drive

If you prefer flexibility, opt for a self-drive trip to explore Coorg at your own pace.
