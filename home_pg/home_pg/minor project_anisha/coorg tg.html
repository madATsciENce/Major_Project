Coorg Tour Guide

Explore the serene landscapes of Coorg with its lush greenery, coffee plantations, and captivating waterfalls!

Day 1: Arrival & Local Exploration

Morning: Arrival in Coorg

Pickup Location: Mysore Airport or Mangalore Airport.

Check-in to your hotel and relax to soak in the beauty of Coorg. Enjoy a welcome drink.

12:00 PM – 4:00 PM: Sightseeing

Visit Abbey Falls, Madikeri Fort, and the local market.

5:00 PM: Evening Leisure

Stroll through the coffee plantations and enjoy a traditional Coorgi meal at a local café.

Day 2: Nature & Adventure

6:00 AM: Trek to Tadiandamol Peak

Start your day with a trek to Tadiandamol Peak, the highest peak in Coorg.

10:00 AM – 12:00 PM: Nature Walk

Enjoy a nature walk through the Dubare Elephant Camp and interact with the majestic elephants.

1:00 PM – 3:00 PM: Coffee Plantation Tour

Take a tour of the coffee plantations and learn about the coffee-making process.

5:00 PM: Return to Hotel

Head back to your hotel and relax, reminiscing about the day's adventures.
