Top Sites to See in Sikkim

Tsomgo Lake

A stunning glacial lake surrounded by snow-capped mountains.

Rumtek Monastery

A spiritual hub and an architectural marvel.

Yumthang Valley

Known as the "Valley of Flowers," ideal for nature lovers.

Gurudongmar Lake

One of the highest lakes in the world, a sight to behold.

Khangchendzonga National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage Site with diverse flora and fauna.

Zuluk Loop

A scenic zigzag road offering mesmerizing views.

Namchi Chardham

A pilgrimage site with magnificent replicas of famous temples.

Pelling Skywalk

India's first glass skywalk with breathtaking views.
