Top Sites to See in Ooty

Doddabetta Peak

The highest peak in the Nilgiris offering breathtaking views.

Ooty Lake

A beautiful lake perfect for boating and picnics.

Botanical Gardens

Home to a wide variety of flora and a perfect picnic spot.

Rose Garden

Features a wide variety of roses and beautiful landscapes.

Nilgiri Mountain Railway

Enjoy a scenic train ride through the mountains.

Tea Museum

Learn about the history of tea in the Nilgiris.

Emerald Lake

A stunning lake surrounded by lush greenery.

Ooty Church

One of the oldest churches in the area, showcasing beautiful architecture.

Kamaraj Sagar Lake

A picturesque lake perfect for a serene escape.

Ooty Market

A vibrant market for local goods and souvenirs.
