Top Sites to See in Lonavala & Khandala

Tiger Point

Offers panoramic views of the valley and waterfalls.

Bhushi Dam

A serene spot for picnics and water splashes.

Rajmachi Fort

An ancient fort with trekking opportunities.

Kune Falls

Majestic waterfalls amidst lush greenery.

Lion's Point

Ideal for sunset views over the valleys.

Karla Caves

Ancient Buddhist caves with intricate carvings.

Bhaja Caves

Historical rock-cut caves.

Duke's Nose

Popular spot for rock climbing.

Ryewood Park

Perfect for families and nature lovers.
