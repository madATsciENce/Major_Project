Top Sites to See in Wayanad

Edakkal Caves

An archaeological site with ancient petroglyphs.

Banasura Sagar Dam

The largest earthen dam in India, surrounded by hills.

Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

Home to diverse flora and fauna, perfect for wildlife lovers.

Soochipara Waterfalls

A stunning waterfall ideal for trekking and swimming.

Chembra Peak

The highest peak in Wayanad, offering trekking opportunities.

Pookode Lake

A beautiful freshwater lake surrounded by forests.
