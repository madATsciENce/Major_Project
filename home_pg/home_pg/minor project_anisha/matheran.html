Top Sites to See in Matheran

Panorama Point

Offers a 360-degree view of the surrounding valleys and the sunrise.

Echo Point

Famous for the natural echoes and stunning views.

Charlotte Lake

A serene spot surrounded by lush greenery.

Louisa Point

Known for its spectacular views of the ancient forts and waterfalls.

Sunset Point

A perfect place to witness the mesmerizing sunset.

Monkey Point

Home to numerous monkeys and a panoramic view of the region.

One Tree Hill

A unique attraction with a single tree on top of the hill.

Alexander Point

Offers a peaceful ambiance and enchanting views.

Garbut Point

Known for its dramatic cliffs and expansive views.
