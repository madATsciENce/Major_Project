Top Sites to See in Darjeeling

Tiger Hill

Watch the sunrise over the Kanchenjunga range from this viewpoint.

Batasia Loop

A scenic railway loop offering panoramic views of the mountains.

Padmaja Naidu Zoological Park

Home to rare Himalayan species, including the red panda and snow leopard.

Himalayan Mountaineering Institute

A training center for mountaineers and a museum dedicated to mountaineering.

Japanese Peace Pagoda

A peaceful pagoda with views of Darjeeling and surrounding hills.

Darjeeling Ropeway

An exciting cable car ride offering views of the tea gardens and valleys.

Happy Valley Tea Estate

Take a tour of this famous tea estate and sample authentic Darjeeling tea.

Rock Garden

A terraced garden with waterfalls and beautiful scenery.

Observatory

A spiritual spot with stunning views, home to a mix of Hindu and Buddhist shrines.

Mahakal Temple

A serene temple located atop Observatory Hill, significant to both Hindus and Buddhists.

Transport Options

Darjeeling is accessible by road from nearby cities like Siliguri. Here are some transport options:

Private Taxi

Book a taxi from Siliguri or Bagdogra Airport for a direct journey to Darjeeling.

Scenic Train Ride

Take a ride on the famous Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Self-Drive

For flexibility, opt for a self-drive trip to explore Darjeeling at your pace.
