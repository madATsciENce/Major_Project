Top Sites to See in Coonoor

Dolphin's Nose

A scenic viewpoint offering panoramic views of the Nilgiri hills and valleys.

Sim's Park

A beautifully landscaped park with diverse flora and a tranquil lake.

Tea Gardens

Experience the lush green tea estates and learn about tea production.

Law's Falls

A stunning waterfall located amidst lush greenery, perfect for a picnic.

Nilgiri Mountain Railway

Enjoy a breathtaking train journey through the picturesque Nilgiri hills.

Ketti Valley

A stunning valley offering spectacular views and a peaceful retreat.

Hidden Valley

A serene location ideal for trekking and exploring nature.

Coonoor Tea Factory

Visit the tea factory to learn about the tea-making process and enjoy tastings.
