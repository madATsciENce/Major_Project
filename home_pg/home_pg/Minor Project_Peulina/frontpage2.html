The Yoga Capital of the World

Rishikesh is a serene blend of spirituality, nature, and adventure. From its ancient temples and yoga ashrams to thrilling river rafting, Rishikesh offers experiences for every traveler.

Top Attractions in Rishikesh

  • Lakshman Jhula - Iconic suspension bridge with stunning views of the Ganges.

  • The Beatles Ashram - Explore the artistic ruins where the Beatles once meditated.

  • Triveni Ghat - Join the evening Ganga Aarti for a spiritual experience.

Best Restaurants in Rishikesh

  • The 60’s Cafe (The Beatles Cafe) - Organic vegetarian food with views of the Ganges.
  • Chotiwala Restaurant - Traditional Indian meals and homely hospitality.
  • Cafe Karma - Laid-back cafe offering Indian and Continental dishes.

Adventure Activities in Rishikesh

  • River Rafting - White-water rafting in the Ganges for thrill-seekers.
  • Bungee Jumping - Jump over the scenic Shivalik Hills for an adrenaline rush.
  • Camping & Trekking - Enjoy camping by the river and exploring mountain trails.
