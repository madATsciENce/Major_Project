Transport Options

Yelagiri is well connected by road. The nearest airport is in Bangalore, and the nearest railway station is in Jolarpettai.

Local transport includes buses, taxis, and auto-rickshaws.

Air Travel

The nearest airport is Kempegowda International Airport, Bangalore, approximately 180 km from Yelagiri.

Rail Travel

Jolarpettai is the closest railway station, around 21 km away, with regular trains from major cities.

Taxi Services

Local taxis and auto-rickshaws are available for hire, providing easy access to local attractions.
