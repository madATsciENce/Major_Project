Transport Options

Yercaud is well connected by road. You can reach here by:

  • Private Car: Drive through scenic routes from nearby cities like Salem. The drive offers beautiful views of the hills and valleys.
  • Bus Services from Salem: Frequent and affordable bus services operate from Salem, making it a convenient option for travelers.
  • Taxi Services: Taxis are readily available for hire from nearby towns, providing a comfortable and flexible way to reach Yercaud.
