Transport Options

Bus Services

Regular bus services operate from major cities like Manali and Shimla to Spiti Valley.

  • Cost: ₹800 - ₹1500
  • Travel Time: Approximately 12-16 hours
  • Tips: Book in advance during peak season for guaranteed seats.

Taxi Rentals

Convenient taxi rentals are available for personalized travel throughout the valley.

  • Cost: ₹3000 - ₹6000 per day
  • Travel Time: Varies based on itinerary
  • Tips: Negotiate the price and confirm inclusions before booking.

Bike Rentals

Experience the thrill of biking through the stunning landscapes of Spiti Valley.

  • Cost: ₹1000 - ₹2000 per day
  • Travel Time: Flexible based on your pace
  • Tips: Ensure you have a valid license and necessary gear.

Shared Jeeps

Shared jeep services are available for budget-friendly travel between popular sites.

  • Cost: ₹500 - ₹800 per person
  • Travel Time: Varies based on routes
  • Tips: Be prepared for a more social experience, as you'll share the ride with others.
