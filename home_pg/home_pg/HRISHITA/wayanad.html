Transport Details

By Car

Self-drive options are available. The roads are well-maintained, making it easy to reach Wayanad.

By Train

The nearest railway station is in Kozhikode, about 85 km away. Taxis are available from the station.

By Flight

The nearest airport is Calicut International Airport, approximately 90 km from Wayanad.
