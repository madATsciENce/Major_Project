    Service Hours: 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM | Rates: Starting at ₹800 per person

  • Airport Pickup and Drop - Convenient pickup and drop services from Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun.

    Service Hours: On-demand | Rates: Starting at ₹1500

  • Local Bus Service - Affordable and regular bus service connecting Rishikesh with Haridwar, Neelkanth, and other attractions.
