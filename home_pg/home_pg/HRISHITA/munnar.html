Transport Options

  • Private Cars and Taxis:

    For a more comfortable journey, hiring a private car or taxi is a great option. Many local taxi services provide pick-up and drop-off from major cities like Kochi and Kottayam. Make sure to book in advance during peak season.

  • State-run Buses:

    Kerala State Road Transport Corporation (KSRTC) operates regular bus services to Munnar from various cities including Kochi, Kottayam, and Ernakulam. Buses are economical and provide a scenic route through the hills.

  • Nearest Railway Station: Ernakulam:

    The nearest major railway station to Munnar is Ernakulam Junction (approximately 130 km away). From there, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Munnar. Several trains connect Ernakulam to major cities in India.

  • Nearest Airport: Cochin International Airport:

    The closest airport to Munnar is Cochin International Airport (COK), about 110 km away. From the airport, you can hire a taxi or book a shuttle service to Munnar. The drive takes approximately 3-4 hours and offers stunning views.
