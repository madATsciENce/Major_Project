Rishikesh Camping & Adventure Tour Package

Embark on a 2-day adventure in the spiritual city of Rishikesh. River rafting, bonfire nights, yoga sessions, and sightseeing await you!

Day 1: Arrival & Adventure Activities

Morning: Pickup & Arrival in Rishikesh

Pickup Location: Delhi/Dehradun (Additional charges for pickup from Delhi).

Arrive at our riverside camp in Rishikesh around 10:00 AM. Enjoy a refreshing drink upon arrival.

11:00 AM – 3:00 PM: River Rafting & Lunch

River Rafting: Experience a thrilling 16 km rafting session on the Ganges, guided by experts.

Lunch: After rafting, enjoy a delicious vegetarian meal at the camp.

4:00 PM – 7:00 PM: Bonfire, Music & Leisure Activities

Bonfire & Music: Relax by the riverside bonfire with light music, tea, and snacks.

Nature Walk: Take a stroll along the river, soaking in the beauty of the surroundings.

8:00 PM: Dinner & Overnight Stay

Enjoy a freshly prepared dinner with multiple vegetarian options. Stay overnight in riverside camps with basic amenities.

Day 2: Exploring Rishikesh & Departure

7:00 AM – 8:00 AM: Yoga & Meditation

Start your day with a rejuvenating yoga session by the Ganges, followed by meditation to relax your mind and soul.

9:00 AM: Breakfast

A healthy breakfast to energize you for a day of sightseeing.

10:00 AM – 12:00 PM: Rishikesh Sightseeing

Explore the following attractions:

  • Lakshman Jhula: Walk across the iconic suspension bridge.
  • Parmarth Niketan: Visit the peaceful ashram known for its evening aarti.
  • Triveni Ghat: Witness the spectacular Ganga Aarti.

12:00 PM – 2:00 PM: Lunch & Free Time

Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant (self-paid). You can explore the local market for souvenirs afterward.

3:00 PM: Departure

Bid farewell to the serene Rishikesh and head back to Delhi/Dehradun with cherished memories.
